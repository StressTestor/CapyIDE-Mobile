privacy policy

last updated: march 4, 2026

CapyIDE ("the app") is a mobile code editor for iOS and Android. this policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

data we collect

we do not collect, store, or transmit your source code. all files you create or edit stay on your device.

API keys

if you configure AI provider API keys (OpenRouter, Groq, Azure OpenAI, Anthropic), those keys are stored locally on your device using platform-native secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences). keys are sent directly to the respective AI provider when you use AI features. we never see or store your keys on any server.

AI requests

when you use AI code assistance, the code context you select is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you configured. we do not proxy, log, or store these requests. refer to each provider's privacy policy for how they handle your data.

advertising

the free version of CapyIDE displays banner ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. you can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. see Google's Privacy Policy for details.

subscriptions

CapyIDE Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). we receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not have access to your payment details. purchase history is used solely to determine whether to show ads.

analytics

we do not use any analytics SDKs. we do not track your usage patterns, screen views, or behavior within the app.

update checks

the app periodically checks GitHub for new releases. this request contains no personal information beyond what a standard HTTPS request includes (IP address, user agent).

children

CapyIDE is not directed at children under 13. we do not knowingly collect data from children.

changes

we may update this policy from time to time. changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
